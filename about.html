ABOUT FYI CLUB

FYI Club, also known as the Fraternity of Young Innovators Club, is a technical club dedicated to being more than just an event organizer. We serve as a safety net for all things technical and academic.

This tenure 25-26, our mission is to redefine the way we experience technical knowledge by moving away from monotonous, conventional events and creating dynamic, engaging, and inclusive spaces. We want innovation and ideas to feel safe, and for everyone to have a genuinely good time while learning.
